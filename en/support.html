Support

Frequently asked questions about Pagewell Nightjar. If you can't find your answer, write to support@simplebuild.it — a real person will reply. Versione italiana.

Do my entries sync between iPhone and iPad?

Yes, automatically, as long as iCloud is enabled on both devices with the same Apple account (System Settings → your name → iCloud). Nothing to configure in the app.

I missed a day — can I write it later?

Yes. From the calendar, select the day and tap "Write for this day"; or, in the editor, tap the date at the top and change it.

How do I protect my journal?

Settings (gear icon, top left) → "Require Face ID". From then on, the journal locks every time the app goes to the background. It also works with your device passcode.

How do I export my data?

Settings → "Export journal": you get a text file with all your entries, to save or share wherever you prefer.

If I delete an entry, is it gone everywhere?

Yes: deletion (with confirmation) propagates to all devices connected to the same iCloud.

The widget isn't updating

The widget updates with every saved entry and at midnight. If it lags behind, remove it and add it to your Home Screen again.

How much does it cost?

Pagewell Nightjar is free.
